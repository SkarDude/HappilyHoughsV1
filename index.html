Our Story

BREAKING NEWS: We just bought a house in Phoenix, Arizona!

Veronica and Alex met in high school AP statistics and AP calculus where they happened to sit next to each other. Alex transferred to Jupiter High School during his senior year and just wanted to stay under the radar. Thinking he was cute, Veronica invited Alex to study. After sea urchin searching in the intracoastal, Alex finally asked Veronica out. Almost 6 years later, here we are. (It was the cookies).

After graduating undergrad, we relocated to Phoenix, Arizona for Veronica to pursue her degree in Veterinary Medicine at Midwestern University. On top of this awesome news Alex was able to begin an amazing career in Software Engineering with a great company.

Along with our dog Lilly and our new puppy Knightro, Veronica and Alex experienced living in the tiny house community. This means we lived in 400sq feet or less and honestly have been loved every moment of it. We decided it was time to upgrade and just purchased a house in Phoenix (For the dogs of course).
